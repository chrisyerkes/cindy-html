Hi, I’m Cindy! I’m a designer with a background in visual communication design and illustration. I also serve as a design professor, where I value empathy, mentorship, and the process of creative exploration. I love the convergence of critical and conceptual thinking and building purposeful design systems. Learning about my clients’ various industries is fascinating, and I enjoy collaborating with them to solve problems through beautiful design and experiences.

Let’s talk! For project inquiries or a quick chat feel free to email: cindylatte06@gmail.com

You can find my comics, drawings, and other studio experiments on instagram.
